Pavé Setting

A pavé necklace is not decorated — it is surfaced. Every millimetre of metal is a decision about stone size, spacing, and the line the eye follows. The arc is drawn first. The stones are mapped to it after.

This piece has no negative space by accident. Where the metal shows, it is meant to show.
